My Hobbies

Here, I have listed down the few things that I do when I feel the need for a well-earned rest. They take the stress away and bring me to a world of peace and joy.

1. Basketball

I first played basketball in 8th grade. It was one of the first times where I was willing to do bad in school just so I could play. I had a lot of fun school-after-hours-memories with my friends that I will treasure forever. Here are some photos of me and my teammates in our high school varsity team.

2. Cubing

Like basketball, I also started getting into cubing during 8th grade. It was when I saw a guy at school do it really fast. On that very day, I bought a cube myself and watched a tutorial. I used to practice multiple hours every day. After 2 years, I was selected to represent the school at a city-wide competition. Fortunately, I was able to win first place. Since then, it became more of a casual thing for me and I've gotten a bit slower. Here's a photo of me holding a cube as well as a 5x5, my favorite puzzle currently.

3. Gaming

Recently, I got into gaming, specifically Valorant. I was just too bored in the ECQ one day that i decided to try it out. I ended up getting addicted to it for a bit. Since the start of classes, I have slowed down on gaming a bit. Here are my 2 favorite agents right now--Omen and Phoenix.

4. Chess

I learned to play chess in 4th grade after a few of my elementary friends taught me how the pieces moved. I found the game so interesting that I convinced my parents to buy me a board and I'd challenge our old neighbors. Because of all this experience, I represented my schools both on the elementary and high school levels. Here's a photo of me playing my last game in the 2017 Division Meet. Also the guy in black & white is Mikhail Tal, my favorite player <3.
