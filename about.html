A Bit About Me

link I am Kian Angelo M. Oladive, a 20-year-old college student from Mandaluyong. I am currently studying Computer Science in the hopes of landing a Software Engineering job in the future, which I think is the line of work that best suits my strengths and skills, and one that I would thoroughly enjoy.


I have always been fond of Math, thus leading me to choose a degree program that is essentially applied mathematics. I chose Computer Science because somewhere along the way, I developed a liking towards tech, and while it isn’t as strong as it once was, the fire is still alive.


I like learning and the challenge that it brings. I have always seen this as one of my key strengths, as this has led me to the pursuit of excellence in whatever I bring my mind to. I believe that my greatest weakness is time management, and this is something that I am always working towards.

Education

Pedro P. Cruz Elementary School

I attended this elementary school from 2007-2013, where I graduated as First Honorable Mention.

City of Mandaluyong Science High School

Apart from being the nearest high school from my house, CMSHS also supposedly bred the most talented and gifted students of the city through its special science and technology curriculum. I had a lot of fun and great memories from MandSci that helped shaped me into the person I am today. High School, to me, was one big learning experience. I graduated both Junior and Senior High School with Honors.

Ateneo de Manila University

I am currently in my 3rd year of Computer Science at Ateneo de Manila University. While it wasn’t my first choice for college in High School, I’m glad I decided on choosing the nurturing, highly learning-conducive, and self-developing environment that Ateneo established within its walls. Choosing Ateneo may just be the best decision of my life thus far. I love it here and I’m excited for what the future holds.
