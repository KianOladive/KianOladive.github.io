About Us

WHO WE ARE


Verdant Agriculture cooperative started as a grain of thought among a small group of overseas Filipino workers in Singapore. As a result of attending Leadership and Social Entrepreneurship Program offered by Ateneo School of Government, it eventually evolves into a well-planned social enterprise cooperative aimed at empowering OFW’s and their families in the Philippines.


What is a Cooperative?


LA CO-OPERATIVE is an autonomous and duly registered association ofpersons with a common bond of interest, who have voluntarily joined together to achieve their social, economic and cultural needs and aspirations by making equitable contributions to the capital required, patronizing their products and services and accepting a fair share of the risks and benefits of the undertaking in accordance with universally-accepted cooperative principles. (Art 3, RA 9520)





Vision and Mission


Vision

  • To create a social enterprise aimed to produce quality and sustainable cacao beans and other agricultural products
  • To provide education and training to OFW’s and families including farmers to produce quality cacao that will be their primary or supplemental source of income and to get rid the remittance dependency of the family to the OFW’s
  • To promote agriculture as a form of profitable business for OFW’s and Families.


Mission


To empower empower OFW and their families, to uplift the socio-economic condition of each member and their community by educating and producing quality cacao and other agriculture related products to supplement the income of OFW’s and to be united with their families the soonest possible
