Gabay's Offices

The Executive Board

The Executive Board comprises half of the Executive Committee. Each of the Executive Board members heads each of the five offices of Ateneo Gabay: Office of the President, Office of the Internals Vice-President, Office of the Externals Vice- President, Office of the Secretary-General, and Office of the Finance Officer. These offices manage the overall systems and protocols that govern various aspects of the entire organization and its members.

Office of the President

The Office of the President oversees the overall direction and functions of the organization. It also oversees the administrative and leadership affairs of Ateneo Gabay.

Office of the Internals Vice President

The Office of the Internals Vice President manages the organization's member relations and member development efforts.

Office of the Externals Vice President

The Office of the Externals Vice President deals with the communication of Gabay's advocacy to external entities and strengthens the organization's ties with its stakeholders.

Office of the Secretary-General

The Office of the Secretary-General is in charge of the inflow, outflow, and preservation of information through the management of Ateneo Gabay's systems.

Office of the Finance Officer

The Office of the Finance Officer oversees the financial acquisition initiatives, as well as manages the funds and cash flows within the financial system of the organization.
