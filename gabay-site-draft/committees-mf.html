Member Formation Committee

Initiatives

Gabay Orientation Seminar

GOrSem is the welcoming event of Ateneo Gabay, which aims to introduce the new members of Ateneo Gabay to the culture, involvements, and foundation established by the organization. It also aims to have a holistic approach in welcoming the new members by introducing them with the activities held inside the organization, the traditions practiced, and the concept of "family" and “pagtataya” for Ateneo Gabay

Chill

CHILL is a sem-long initiative of the Member Formation Committee. This project consists of weekly group meetings with other Gabayanos facilitated by a CHILL head. Following a set of modules, the meetings revolve around themes like reflection, sharing insights, relaxation, and taking the time to escape from the noise of life.

Talent Week

Talent Week is a week-long project that aims to showcase the different talents of Gabayanos and share it with the community. Ateneo Gabay is indeed lucky to have members who are full of talent and passion that should be shared with the world.

Talent Pools

The three (3) talent pools of the Member Formation Committee consist of: Gabay Band (GaBand), Gabay Awesome Dancers (GADs), and Gabay Hosts (GHosts) all aiming to provide entertainment-related activities such as singing, dancing, and hosting to projects in the organization.

Content Management Pool

This pool aims to provide a much more strategic approach in managing all the content posted online to make sure the online presence of Ateneo Gabay is being maintained. This pool offers content consultation, content ideation, and fun gimmicks like mini-vlogs, podcasts, and many more!
