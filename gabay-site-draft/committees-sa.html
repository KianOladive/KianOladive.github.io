Scholars' Adjustment Committee

Initiatives

Scholars’ Support Group

The Scholars' Support Group is a year-long project that serves to welcome new scholars into the Ateneo by offering avenues where they can get to know each other and learn about the vibrant scholar community. Launched in partnership with the Academics committee during Salubong, almost everyone finds their first college barkada in SSG.

Gabby the Scholar

Your friendly online scholar buddy who is always ready to help! Gabby would not be around without the team working behind it, making sure that scholar concerns are addressed as soon as possible and every scholar stays updated with the latest information all year round.

Kuwentong Scholar

Every scholar has a story to tell and that's exactly what Kuwentong Scholar highlights! Another year-long initiative under the SA Committee, KS releases materials in various forms (comics, posters, videos, etc.) that tug at your heartstrings as you find yourself in each unique scholar experience.

Scholars’ Week

The gift of scholarship deserves to be celebrated--which is why we devote a special week to do just that! Composed of various events and activities, SWeek is an avenue for scholars to showcase their talents, gather as a community, and inspire others with their stories.
